# Helios Service Level Agreement Policy

Synthetic fixture document (fictional customer) for the Orionfold Advisor
corpus-pack swap gate.

## Availability

The advisor API carries a 99.5% monthly availability target. Planned
maintenance windows are excluded when announced 72 hours in advance.

## Support tiers

- Standard support answers tickets within 2 business days.
- Priority support answers within 4 business hours and includes a quarterly
  model-quality review.

## Data retention

Raw telemetry is retained on-site for 13 months. Aggregated state-of-health
summaries are retained for the contract lifetime. Helios never transmits raw
telemetry off-site; only anonymized model metrics leave the network boundary,
and only when the customer enables the telemetry-sharing flag.

## Credits

If monthly availability falls below 99.5%, the customer receives a 10% service
credit; below 98%, the credit is 25%.
